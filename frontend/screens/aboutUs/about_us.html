Our Story

Founded in 2025, STOKKO started with a simple observation: the grocery market lacked transparency. Buyers struggled to find fair prices, and sellers had limited tools to understand market trends and optimize their inventory.

We built STOKKO to bridge this gap. Our platform combines real-time price intelligence, verified sellers, and powerful analytics to create a marketplace where everyone wins.

Today, we serve thousands of buyers and sellers across Indonesia, processing millions in transactions every month. But we're just getting started.
